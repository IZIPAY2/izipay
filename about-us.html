About us

We represent a service constructed with care and high regard to our customer needs and security.

IZI Pay was founded with the goal of relieving crypto holders use of cryptocurrencies in their daily lives without complicating transaction procedure and automatically converting crypto balance to fiat at the point of transaction. As we value our customers privacy - one of the key reasons why you might want to use cryptocurrency in our opinion, there is an option to disregard KYC and use our product with certain few limitations.

Licensed operations, convenience in using our application and users trust combined give us confidence in far-reaching potential of our efforts in making the service better and multifunctional.

Our team strives to align your goals with ours and we are happy to receive feedback at every stage of your user experience and we hope that you will enjoy our product!
